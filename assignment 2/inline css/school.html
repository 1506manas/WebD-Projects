KENDRIYA VIDYALAYA MAU

For decades, they have been the best the government system can offer in schooling. The Kendriya Vidyalayas were established in 1963 with the sole purpose of offering quality education to the children of transferable central government employees, including those in the defence and paramilitary services.

It is a system of central government schools in India and other countries that have been instituted under the aegis of the Ministry of Human Resource Development and came into being in 1963 under the name Central Schools. Later, the name was changed to Kendriya Vidyalaya. It is a non-profit organisation.
